About Us

Welcome to Trade News Network (TNN), your trusted partner in sector-specific news excellence. At TNN, we believe in the power of news to transform brands and industries alike. Our journey is rooted in a legacy of PR and media expertise, making us a go-to destination for precision-targeted reach, expert content creation, and strategic connections.

Our story

The TNN Story is one of innovation, diversity, and unwavering commitment to sector-driven news excellence. Founded by Vinay Maurya, TNN was born out of a vision to bridge the gap between brands and sector-specific news coverage. With years of experience in the PR & Media industry, we recognized the need for a platform that not only delivers news on multiple platforms but also elevates brands within their industries.

Our Vision

At TNN, our vision is to be the foremost catalyst for sector-specific growth and brand success. We aspire to empower industries with strategic connections and unparalleled insights, making every brand thrive through the power of sector-driven news excellence. Our diverse portfolio of dedicated news publications ensures that brands receive the spotlight they deserve in their respective sectors.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect, inform, and inspire. We are dedicated to delivering sector-specific news excellence that empowers brands and drives industry impact. We aim to provide a one-stop solution for precision-targeted reach, connecting brands to industry bodies, associations, councils, experts, and policymakers. With a relentless focus on diversity and innovation, we navigate the ever-evolving landscape of diverse sectors, delivering singular excellence in every dimension.

Navigating B2B News with Finesse

At Trade News Network, we've carefully crafted our logo to convey our commitment to guiding you through the dynamic world of B2B news. It's more than just an emblem; it's a symbol of our dedication to delivering comprehensive coverage from every corner of the business landscape.

The Compass:

At the heart of our logo, you'll find a compass—a symbol chosen with precision. Just as a compass provides direction, we too provide guidance in the world of news. But there's more to it than that.

NEWS in Every Direction:

Our compass is a visual representation of the saying that the word "NEWS" is an abbreviation of North, East, West, and South. It embodies our mission to provide news from all directions, ensuring you're well-informed about the latest developments in every sector and industry.

Icons of Diversity:

Each directional point of the compass features icons representing a variety of sectors, including Healthcare, Media & Entertainment, Sports, Technology, E-commerce, Automobile, Real Estate, Education, Business, and BFSI. These icons showcase our diverse range of B2B news portals, each dedicated to a specific industry.

TNN at the Core:

Our logo wouldn't be complete without "TNN" creatively placed at its center. It signifies our identity—Trade News Network—a hub where all these sectors converge to bring you the latest and most relevant news. Our logo encapsulates our essence: a dynamic network of B2B news publications guiding you through every industry. Welcome to Trade News Network, where comprehensive coverage knows no bounds.
