Careers

Why Join Us?

Join our dynamic team at Trade News Network, where innovation meets passion, and individual growth is part of our collective success. As an agency that prides itself on media intelligence, versatile PR solutions, and consistent delivery, we are continuously on the lookout for talented individuals who can add value to our mission and vision.

Dynamic Work Environment:
We provide a vibrant, fast-paced environment that encourages creative thinking, strategic problem-solving, and collaborative teamwork.

Growth and Learning: We are committed to the professional development of our team. At Trade News Network, you will have opportunities to work across sectors, gain deep insights into the media landscape, and accelerate your growth trajectory.

Impactful Work: We work with clients right from their ideation stage to becoming leaders in their respective sectors. Being a part of our team means your work will have a significant impact on brands and their success stories.

Culture of Excellence: We strive for consistent delivery and quality in everything we do. This pursuit of excellence shapes our work ethic and company culture.

If you are a motivated individual with a knack for strategic thinking, media relations, or crafting compelling narratives, we would love to hear from you. Join our team and let's redefine the boundaries of communication together.

To apply, please send your resume to hr@tradenewsnetwork.in with the job title as the subject line.

Trade News Network is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
